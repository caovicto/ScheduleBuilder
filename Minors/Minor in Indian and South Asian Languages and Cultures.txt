Requirement 1: Minor - Indian and South Asian Languages and Cultures - Language Proficiency
Minor - Indian and South Asian Languages and Cultures - Language Proficiency: 1 course from equivelant of 2.5 years of academic study in a South Asian language or demonstrate proficiency in South Asian language through placement testing.
Requirement 2: Minor - Indian and South Asian Languages and Cultures - Required Elective
Minor - Indian and South Asian Languages and Cultures - Required Elective: 17 credits from ASN291, ASN491, ENG360, HA260, HA261, HA461, MUS425, REL340, REL345, REL350
Conditions:
Minor - Indian and South Asian Languages and Cultures - Required Elective: Students must obtain advisor approval before enrolling in ASN 291, ASN 491, ENG 360, and HA 461 to ensure appropriate South Asian content. (Applies to Requirement(s) 2)
