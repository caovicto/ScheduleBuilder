Requirement 1: Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Foreign Language Proficiency
Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Foreign Language Proficiency: 1 course from Modern foreign language to demonstrate proficiency at a level equivalent to four semesters at the university level.
Requirement 2: Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Conceptual Coursework
Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Conceptual Coursework: 2 courses from ABM427, ANP321, ANP370, ANP431, ANP436, ANR250, ANR475, ANS480, EC410, EEP260, GEO435, GEO454, MC320, PLS342, PLS344, PLS347, PLS363, SOC310, SOC362, SOC416, SOC451, SOC452, WS403
Requirement 3: Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Regional Coursework
Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Regional Coursework: A total 1 course from 1 of the following sets of courses
Africa and the Middle East
Asia.
Eastern Europe.
Latin America and the Caribbean.
Requirement 4: Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Fieldwork Experience
Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Fieldwork Experience: A total 3 credits from 1 of the following sets of courses
A domestic internship that involves working on global issues.
An overseas field experience or internship in one of four geographic areas listed in requirement V4.
A thesis or independent study under the direction of a faculty member.
Requirement 5: Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Capstone
Minor - Global Studies in Social Science - Capstone: 1 course from Senior Level Seminar or Capstone Course with significant International Development content approved by academic advisor.
